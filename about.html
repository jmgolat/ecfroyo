Introduction

Eau Claire Froyo is a frozen yogurt shop located in Eau Claire, Wisconsin. our establishment is dedicated to providing a family friendly environment for people of all ages to come in and have a refreshing desert. Frozen yogurt is the healthier alternative to ice cream and froyo allows for a lot more ways to customize a sundae with a variety of topping selections! Froyo is also self-serve, which allows you to pick the exact amount of everything that you want to eat. Perfect just for you!

History

We are new to the Chippewa Valley! Our founder Jackson Frost began his froyo journey back in 2013 where he grew passionate about the tasty treat while working for a froyo shop in his home town. Ever since, we wanted to build his own froyo shop! This gave rise to Eau Claire froyo which just opened its doors!
